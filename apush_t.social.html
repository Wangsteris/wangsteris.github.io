Social Media in Modern Society

Social Media plays a major role in news reporting.

Due to their simplicity in usage and vast availablitiy, often times witnesses could report on news faster and to a deeper level of depth as traditional media could.

Because of this, American society has developed into a society where social media became a major source of news.
